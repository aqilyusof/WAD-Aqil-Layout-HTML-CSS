Favorite Websites

W3Schools
Learn HTML/CSS

W3Schools is one of the most popular platforms for learning web development. It offers beginner-friendly tutorials, coding examples, and interactive exercises for HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. I often use this site to strengthen my web development skills and to practice coding directly in the browser.

Canva
Design tool

Canva is an online design platform that helps users create graphics, posters, resumes, social media posts, and more. I use Canva to design creative posters for assignments and social media content, especially because it offers professional templates even for non-designers like me.

Twitch
Gaming streams
Twitch is a live-streaming platform where users broadcast and watch gameplay, talk shows, music, and more. I follow streamers not only for entertainment but also to observe how content creators engage with their audience—something that inspires me in terms of digital communication and branding.
Behance
Creative showcase
Behance is a platform by Adobe where designers and creatives showcase their portfolios. I explore Behance to get design inspiration and ideas for layout, web design, and presentation formats. It's also useful to see how professionals structure and present their projects to clients.
